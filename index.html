Wind Farm Analysis

2025 - 2026

Modelled the annual capacity factor of the Post Oak Wind LLC plant (Abilene, TX) - 98 × Gamesa G87-2.0 turbines, 200 MW - using the actuator disk model and hourly wind data.

The model gave a rotor-shaft capacity factor of 21.77% against EIA-923 gross data of 30.40% (39.74% adjusted for drivetrain losses), with the gap attributed mainly to Cp simplification. A parametric sweep found uprating each turbine to 6.5 MW maximises annual energy yield; beyond that, capacity factor falls monotonically.

CFD Convection Scheme Solver

2025 - 2026

Implemented and benchmarked three FVM discretisation schemes - CDS, UDS, and PLDS - for the steady 1-D convection-diffusion equation in Python, solved via the Tri-Diagonal Matrix Algorithm and validated against the analytical solution.

CDS is accurate at Pe < 2 but diverges above that threshold; UDS is unconditionally stable but introduces numerical diffusion causing a systematic offset (error 0.5–5%); PLDS offers the best compromise, remaining stable across all conditions with errors of 0.001–1%. An optimal local Pe ≈ 4.4 minimises PLDS error, and a non-uniform grid refined near boundaries improves accuracy across all three schemes.

C-130 Hercules CFD Analysis

2025 - 2026

RANS-based CFD simulation of external aerodynamic flow around the Lockheed C-130 Hercules in STAR-CCM+, comparing k-ε, SST k-ω, and Reynolds Stress Model turbulence approaches across angle-of-attack and take-off studies on a 1.8M-cell half-domain mesh.

Grid independence was confirmed below 0.4% change in C L and C D. k-ε predicts stall at 13.75°, RSM at 11.5°, SST k-ω at 10.25° — k-ε delays separation by overpredicting turbulent mixing via its isotropic Boussinesq assumption. RSM gave the most physically credible prediction (take-off: 83.2 m/s at max payload); SST k-ω offers the best accuracy-to-cost trade-off.

Concentrating Solar Power Market Study

2024 - 2025

Literature review assessing CSP's impact on the renewable energy market through a comparative analysis with photovoltaics, examining the economic, technical, and socio-political factors behind the divergence between the two technologies.

By 2023, PV held 36.5% of global renewable capacity against CSP's 0.2%. CSP's LCOE plateaued at ~$0.12/kWh post-COVID while PV's fell to $0.044/kWh; stricter siting, high water use, and poor scalability widen the gap. Hybrid CSP + PV plants — with modelled LCOE reductions up to 21% — are the most viable path forward.

2D Wave Equation Solver

2023 - 2024

Python solver modelling sound wave propagation in a confined 2D room using the explicit finite-difference method on the 2D wave equation, with a Gaussian point source, Dirichlet reflecting-wall boundaries, and 3D surface, contour, and mid-plane slice visualisations.

The stability criterion r = k²/h² ≤ ½ ties the time step to the mesh; at the limit the stencil simplifies to u ⁿ⁺¹ = ½(four neighbours) − u ⁿ⁻¹. On a 70-interval grid (compute time 8.91 s) the solver reproduces wall reflections and interference correctly; a 50× refinement at fixed k diverges, exposing the fundamental stability – cost trade-off.
